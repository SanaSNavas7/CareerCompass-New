About Career Compass

Career Compass is designed to be your personal guide in navigating the complex world of career development. Our mission is to empower job seekers and professionals by providing insightful analysis and actionable recommendations to help them achieve their career goals.

What We Offer:

  • Skill Analysis: Upload your resume and get a breakdown of your existing skills. Our system uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to compare these against the requirements of various job roles.
  • AI-Powered Personalized Recommendations: Based on your skills and chosen target roles, our AI generates highly personalized suggestions for career paths and areas for skill improvement.
  • AI Chatbot: Our intelligent AI chatbot is available to answer a wide range of career-related questions and provide dynamic advice.
  • Learning Resources: We point you to valuable online resources to help you acquire new skills and enhance your professional profile.

Our Approach (Hybrid AI & NLP):

In this version of Career Compass, we combine the power of advanced AI models with efficient local NLP processing:

  • Skill Extraction (using spaCy): We utilize the spaCy library to process your resume text, identify key terms, and match them against a comprehensive list of known skills. This provides a robust and localized skill identification.
  • AI-Powered Recommendations (using Gemini API): For personalized career path and skill improvement suggestions, we leverage the Google Gemini API. This allows for highly dynamic and contextually rich advice generation.
  • AI Chatbot (using Gemini API): Our chatbot also utilizes the Google Gemini API to provide real-time, intelligent, and conversational responses to your career queries.
  • Static Job Skill Sets: We maintain a predefined database of key skills required for various common job roles to ensure consistent matching.

This hybrid approach ensures both intelligent, dynamic interactions where needed, and efficient, reliable processing for core resume analysis.
